Campus Life

Tennis Court

Two tennis courts conforming to global standards, made of synthetic grass and long lasting surface requiring no maintenance are provided.

Ideal for clubs and schools which use the court frequently, it has been classified as Category 4 medium fast court by International Tennis Federation.

Science Lab

The school is well equipped with Physics, Chemistry and Biology labs, which adhere to the National Curriculum Framework.

Hands on science projects are interwoven with theoretical learning and practiced in our science labs by primary and middle school students.
